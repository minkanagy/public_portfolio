Destin

Mobile App iOS Design UI/UX Figma

A location-based social app for sharing and discovering authentic place recommendations from friends. Destin combines visual reviews with personal ratings to help users find the best spots recommended by people they trust.

About the Project

Destin reimagines how we discover and share places. Instead of relying on anonymous reviews, users see recommendations from their social circle with authentic photos and personal ratings.
