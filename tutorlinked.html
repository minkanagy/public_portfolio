A peer-to-peer tutoring marketplace initially developed for UC Berkeley students with potentail for expansion to other universities. Through the platform, students can find verified peer tutors, book sessions, use real-time messaging, and pay securely.

About the Project

TutorLinked connects students with peer tutors who recently completed the same courses, creating a more authentic and accessible alternative to traditional tutoring services.

The platform uses a connection-based privacy model. Before being able to see availabilities for a tutor and book sessions, students must send a connection request. After the connection request is accepted, students can book tutors during their marked availabilities. Following a session, students can leave reviews and ratings for tutors, which are displayed on the tutor's profile. Payments for sessions are administered through Stripe Connect.

Tech Stack

React/TypeScript frontend on Vercel, Node.js/Express backend on Render, and MongoDB Atlas for the database. Real-time features via Socket.io, payments via Stripe Connect, auth via Google OAuth, and transactional emails via Resend.
